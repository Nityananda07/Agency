Beauty

"The sun does not shine for a few trees and flowers, but for the wide world's joy."

- Henry Ward Beecher

Amidst whispers of leaves, nature's tapestry unfolds,
A symphony of colors, a story never told.
Mountains touch the heavens with majestic grace,
Rivers weave tales as they rhythmically trace.


Sunsets paint the skies in hues of gold and fire,
Stars above twinkle, fulfilling dreams and desire.
Flowers bloom in fields, a fragrant delight,
Morning dew kisses petals, in dawn's soft light.


Nature's beauty, a masterpiece divine,
A gift to cherish, an eternal design.
